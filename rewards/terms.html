Bro Rewards — Program Rules

Last updated: May 2026

1. Eligibility

The Bro Rewards program is open to anyone with a valid BroTrading Rewards account. By creating an account you agree to these program rules. BroTrading reserves the right to suspend or permanently remove participants in the event of suspected fraud or abuse.

2. Earning points

Points are awarded for the following actions:

  • Purchase Bro Points — purchases made at participating prop firms through a BRO affiliate link earn Bro Points based on the account type purchased.
  • Daily login bonus — logging in and claiming your daily bonus each day builds a login streak. Milestone bonuses are awarded at day 7 and day 30.
  • Referral bonus — when someone signs up using your referral link and has their first purchase claim approved, you earn bonus points.
  • Reviews — submitting an approved firm review earns bonus points.
  • Welcome bonus — a one-time bonus awarded when you create your account.

Points have no monetary value and cannot be redeemed for cash.

3. Purchase claims

The following conditions apply to every purchase claim:

  • The purchase must have been made through an official BRO affiliate link or using the BRO discount code. Purchases made directly on the prop firm's website without a traceable BRO referral are not eligible.
  • The purchase date must not be earlier than your account registration date.
  • Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date. Older claims will not be accepted.
  • Only one pending claim per firm is allowed at a time.
  • You must provide a valid order or confirmation number and a proof of purchase (a screenshot or PDF of your order confirmation).
  • All claims are reviewed manually. BroTrading reserves the right to reject a claim if the information provided is incomplete, inaccurate, or suspicious.

4. Redeeming points

Points can be redeemed for rewards listed in the Bro Rewards catalog. The following conditions apply:

  • You must have earned a minimum number of points from approved purchases before you can redeem any reward. This threshold ensures the program benefits active users only.
  • Rewards are subject to availability. BroTrading reserves the right to update the catalog at any time.
  • Once points have been redeemed they will not be refunded. If a reward cannot be delivered for logistical reasons, an equivalent alternative will be offered.

5. Fraud and abuse

The Bro Rewards program is intended for personal use only. The following activities are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal from the program:

  • Creating multiple accounts to accumulate points or exploit referral bonuses.
  • Submitting false or manipulated proofs of purchase.
  • Claiming Bro Points on purchases that were not made through a BRO affiliate link.
  • Any other form of manipulation of the points system.

If abuse is detected, all points on the account in question will be forfeited and the account may be permanently banned.

6. General

BroTrading reserves the right to modify these program rules, the points system, earn rates, and the catalog at any time. Changes will be announced on the website. Continued use of the Bro Rewards program after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

The Bro Rewards program is not a financial product. Points are non-transferable, non-tradeable, and will expire after an extended period of account inactivity.

For questions or support, reach out via the BroTrading Discord.
